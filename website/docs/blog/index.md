## Where It All Began

<figure markdown="span">
  ![Jamal the plush octopus](../assets/images/jamal-the-original.jpg){ width="380" loading=lazy }
  <figcaption>Jamal — the plush octopus that started a whole engineering project</figcaption>
</figure>

This project exists because of a plush octopus named **Jamal**, found in a TEDi discount bin during a random shopping trip. On the walk home, my wife Emma and I gave him a name, a personality, and an entire fictional inner life — sassy, opinionated, suspiciously comfortable in our armchair. The natural next question: *what would it take to actually build a living, responsive, digital version of this guy?* That question became Dilder. [Read the full origin story on the home page.](../index.md#the-origin-story--how-jamal-was-found)
